- **Pros:** Reduces your support load (AI handles 80% of queries); cloneable for all apps, enhancing user experience.
- **Cons/Refinements:** AI limitations (e.g., off-topic responses)—mitigate with prompt engineering (e.g., "Stay factual; if unsure, escalate"). Test with role-playing scenarios. Timeline: 1 day, since AI is already pluggable.

Overall: This elevates MuseKit from "starter" to "support-ready"—love it.
